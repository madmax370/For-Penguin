02 July 💜
Dear Bhandhari!

Assalamualaikum. 🌸

Happiest Birthday, Bhandhari. ❤️

Aaj ka din mere liye bhi bahut special hai, kyunki aaj us insaan ka birthday hai jiski muskurahat dekh kar mujhe duniya me sabse zyada sukoon milta hai. Sach kahu, tujhe khush aur haste hue dekh kar jo ehsaas hota hai, usse main kabhi lafzon mein bayan nahi kar sakta.

Main dil se dua karta hu ke Allah tujhe hamesha khush rakhe, sehatmand rakhe, lambi aur barkat wali zindagi ata farmaye, aur har qadam par tera saath de. Main chahta hu ke tu apni life mein itna aage badhe ke ek din tujhe khud par fakhr ho. Kabhi kisi cheez se darna mat. Allah tere dil ki har achhi dua aur har nek khwahish ko poora kare. Main chahta hu ke tu jis field mein bhi jaaye, usmein bahut kamyab ho, aur apne Ammi Abba ke har sapne ko poora kare. Mujhe yakeen hai ke tu ek din bahut aage jayegi, In Sha Allah.

Aur agar kabhi bhi life mein kisi cheez mein meri zarurat pade, chahe studies ho, koi problem ho, ya bas kisi se baat karni ho, to yaad rakhna, main hamesha apni taraf se tere saath khada rahunga. Main hamesha teri khushi aur kamyabi ki dua karta rahunga.

Agar maine kabhi bhi jaane ya anjaane mein tera dil dukhaya ho, to dil se maafi chahta hu. Kabhi mera irada tujhe dukh pahunchane ka nahi tha.

Ek baat jo shayad main kabhi theek se bol nahi paaya. Shayad main teri life mein utni importance na rakhta hu jitni tu meri life mein rakhti hai. Lekin mere liye tu hamesha meri life ke sabse khaas aur special logon mein se ek rahegi.

Meri bas itni si dua hai ke Allah tujhe har buri nazar se mehfooz rakhe. Kabhi tere chehre ki muskurahat kam na ho, kabhi teri aankhon mein gham ke aansu na aaye. Allah tujhe hamesha sukoon, izzat, kamyabi aur barakah se nawaze. Chahe meri jagah teri zindagi mein ho ya na ho, main hamesha Allah se yehi dua karunga ke tu hamesha khush rahe, sehatmand rahe, aur har din pehle se zyada kamyab hoti rahe.

Allah tujhe duniya aur aakhirat dono ki har khair, har khushi aur har barkat ata farmaye. Ameen. 🤲

Once again, Happiest Birthday, Bhandhari. 🤍

Apna hamesha khayal rakhna, aur hamesha isi tarah muskurati rehna. Teri muskurahat mere liye duniya ki sabse khoobsurat cheezon mein se ek hai. ✨

~ Tera Apna Bhatari 🤍
