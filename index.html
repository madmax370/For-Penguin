Dear Bhandhari!

Assalamualaikum. 🤍

Bas ek baat poochni thi... Tu theek hai na?

Agar maine jaan-boojh kar ya anjaane mein koi aisi baat kar di jis se tera dil dukha ho, toh dil se I'm really sorry. Mera kabhi bhi intention tujhe hurt karne ka nahi tha.

Agar tu mujhse naraz hai, toh bas ek baar bata de meri galti kya thi. Main sirf samajhna chahta hoon, taaki apni galti sudhaar sakun. Agar kisi aur wajah se tu pareshan hai, toh bhi dua karta hoon ke Allah tujhe har takleef se mehfooz rakhe aur sab aasaan kar de.

Main tujhe force nahi karunga, aur na hi disturb karunga. Bas itna chahta hoon ke jab tera dil kare, ek baar baat kar lena. Kabhi-kabhi ek chhoti si baat bhi saari ghalat-fehmiyan door kar deti hai.

Waise bhi... agar tu hi baat nahi karegi, toh tere is hawle dost ke bekaar jokes pe hasi kaun udaayega? 😅 Aur agar main hi nahi raha baat karne ke liye, toh teri speaker jaisi awaaz ka free concert kaun sunega? 😂

Tu meri nazron mein hamesha ek achhi dost rahegi, aur main nahi chahta ke hamare beech khamoshi ya narazgi reh jaaye.

Apna khayal rakhna. Allah tumhe hamesha khush rakhe.

Aur agar ho sake... toh mujhe maaf kar dena. 🤍

~ Bhatari 🤍
