Assalamualaikum Warahmatullahi Wa Barakatuh 🤍

Please Bhandhari, ye letter dimag se nahi, dil se padhna… aur achhe se padhna, apna waqt lekar. ✨

Tere liye maine ye chota sa part likha tha… tu jab gaaon jaari thi na, us din likha tha ye:

Tujhe yaad hai tuition me ek baar main teri black book me likh raha tha, but tune boli thi:
“Tum mere kon bhi nahi lagte” something…
Sahi me woh baat mujhe bhot hurt huwi thi. 💔

But jab hamari itni friendship nahi thi, isiliye tu bhi sahi thi jab.

Then, main tujhse 2 din baat nahi kiya tha, phir tune sorry boli thi… us din mere liye tu sab se best aur sab se special person bangayi.. 🌸

Kyuki first time kisi ne mujhe manaya tha… and wo person tu thi. 🤍

Aur yaad hai, tu ne ek baar teri place bhi change ki thi kyuki tujhe lagta tha main tujhe disturb karne ka… wo Shaheda ke waha ja kar baithne ki… phir 2 din baad phirse mere paas aake baithne lagi 😅

Hum tuition me kaise ek dusre ke liye book me letter likhne ke na… main bhot kam likhne ka letter me, to tu bolne ki mujhe 3 pages ka likh kar do, itna kam kyu likhte. 📖

Mujhe jab bhi teri yaad aati hai na, to tere letters read karta hu… wo teri drawing bhi abhi tak hai mere paas — wo 2 hands banaye thi tune, cute banayi thi bhot. 🫶

Tu mere baazu baithne ke liye ghar ke darwaze se hi book pass karne ki… ke waha par rakho, jagah na chali jaaye isiliye. 🥹

Wo tera baat baat par mujhe maarna… aaj bhi yaad hai mujhe.
Tere faltu jokes par hasna… aaj bhi yaad hai mujhe. 😄

Mujhe teri har choti si choti baate yaad hai… jisse main bhulane se nahi bhul paayonga.

Tere sath mere itne haseen pal guzre hai mere… usme se sab se best moment tha mere liye, jab hum sab movie dekhre the na, toh tune mera hath pakdi thi… 🤍

Tera hath mere hathon par aaj bhi mehsoos hota hai...

You were the best and last thing Allah gave me in my life.
I don't want to lose you in any chance. 🌙

Mujhe teri har ek khwaahish ko pura karna hai, Inshallah. ✨

Maana ke khoobsurat nahi hoon main,
Par tera khayal khoobsurti se rakhunga ~ Meri Bhandhari. 🤍

Ye sab maine likha tha us din… tu chahe to aur likhunga tere liye. ✍️

Kyuki aapki tareef me lafz kam pad jaate hai.

Aur sirf main hi likhu… tu nahi likhengi kuch? 🥺

Aur tune meri request ko do baar nazarandaz kiya… maine teri bas ek pic maanga tha, aur woh bhi mujhe nahi de rahe ho 😫

Kya karu main 256GB storage ka agar usme teri photos hi na ho toh. 📱💔

To kuch kripya karke hamari bhi is wish ko puri kardijiye... ✨

~ Tera Apna Bhatari 🤍
